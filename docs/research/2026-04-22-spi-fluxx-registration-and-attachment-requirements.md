# 2026-04-22: ISOC SPI Fluxx registration + attachment requirements (verified)

## Context

RISE submitted organization registration to the ISOC Foundation Grants Portal (isocfoundation.fluxx.io) today for the Sustainable Peering Infrastructure (SPI) 2026 round, Peering Ecosystem Support track.

This doc captures (a) the registration flow as experienced, (b) a verified correction to the attachment list in prior research, and (c) a for-profit eligibility confirmation.

## Registration flow

- Gio completed the Organization Registration form and submitted.
- Portal displayed "Thank you for submitting your information" confirmation page.
- Email received from ISOC Foundation stating staff will review within 1-2 business days. Two follow-up emails will arrive from @fluxx.io addresses: (1) review completion notification, (2) login + password reset request.
- No attachments requested at registration stage.
- A short polite expedite email was sent to grants@isoc.foundation flagging the 23 April 21:00 UTC deadline. Administrative chase, not project pitch.

## Attachment requirements (CORRECTED vs prior research)

Prior research doc `docs/research/2026-04-16_isoc-spi-2026-program-research.md` listed Peering Ecosystem Support required documents as:
- Completed budget template
- Completed workplan template
- Minimum one document demonstrating community interest

WebFetch of https://www.isocfoundation.org/grant-programme/sustainable-peering-infrastructure/ on 2026-04-22 returns the authoritative list:
- Clear responses to all application questions
- **Budget, Workplan, AND Equipment Request (single combined template provided)**
- At least 1 letter or document that demonstrates community interest and/or need

Differences from prior:
- Budget + Workplan + Equipment Request are **one combined template**, not three separate files.
- Equipment Request is a new component. For IXPerience Philippines (a workshops program with no equipment purchases), this section is expected to be N/A.
- Template is served inside the Fluxx application form after login, not on the general templates-and-resources page.

## For-profit eligibility

Verified on the same program page: the nonprofit restriction applies only to the IXP Support track, not the Peering Ecosystem Support track. RISE is eligible without caveats.
