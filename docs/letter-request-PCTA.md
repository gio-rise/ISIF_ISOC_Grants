Subject: Request for Letter of Support — ISOC Sustainable Peering Infrastructure Grant Application

Dear PCTA Leadership,

I hope this message finds you well.

Rise is preparing an application to the Internet Society Foundation's Sustainable Peering Infrastructure (SPI) Grant Program for 2026. The application is for the Peering Ecosystem Support track (up to USD 25,000) and proposes a series of regional peering-community workshops across the Philippines, building on our existing IXperience program.

As you know, PCTA has been a valued community partner of IXperience since 2025, helping us reach cable operators and ISPs across the provinces. The SPI-funded project would expand this work by adding structured peering-community meetups in provincial cities, seeding regional chapters, and onboarding more provincial operators to IXP peering.

We would be grateful if PCTA could provide a brief letter of support for our application. The letter does not commit PCTA to any financial obligation — it simply confirms:

1. PCTA's role as a community partner of the IXperience workshop series
2. That PCTA members (provincial cable operators and ISPs) would benefit from expanded peering-community workshops outside Metro Manila
3. PCTA's willingness to continue supporting the program through member outreach and event promotion
